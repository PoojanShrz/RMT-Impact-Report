Bhaktapur Panauti Day Trip

This 6-hour tour begins from Kathmandu and takes its first sightseeing intermission at a legendary town, Bhaktapur. The highlights of this protected medieval city are traditional pottery, wood carving, beautiful temples, ponds and old traditional wooden houses. The tour continues to Panauti, a small village surrounded by green hills and holy rivers, that embodies the spirit of an ancient Newari community. While UNESCO world heritage site Bhaktapur showcases the best of Newari architectural skills, Panauti offers travellers an authentic experience of Newari hospitality and an opportunity to enjoy a local home-cooked meal.
