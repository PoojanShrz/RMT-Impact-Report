Our commitment
for 2020

1 2 3 4 5 6 7

Add more electric vehicles in our fleet

Since smaller cars can carry less people compared to bigger vehicles, the per capita carbon footprint for a small vehicle is higher. That is why we want to start replacing the smaller vehicles in our fleet with electric vehicles

Become carbon neutral in our operations

This year we have measured the carbon emissions from our operations. In the coming year, we will reduce our emissions and offset the remaining emissions to become carbon neutral.

Discuss retirement plans with older guides

As some of the guides in our network get older, we plan to discuss their retirement plans and identify areas where we can support them.

Promote more community-based products

As community-based tourism gets more traction around the world, we plan to develop and offer more exciting products in this area.

Train and appoint more female tour leaders

Next year we want to work towards increasing representation of women not only in our offices but also out in the fields.

Analyze the sustainability performance of all of our key suppliers and provide recommendations

We understand that while it is important for us to become a sustainable tour operator, we also need to influence other companies in our network to become more sustainable.

Conduct interactive sessions with guides on the topic of how to become more sustainable on the field.

We are as committed to becoming more sustainable on the field as we are to becoming more sustainable in our office operations. We plan to conduct regular interactive sessions with our tour leaders to become more sustainable overall.
